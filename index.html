About the Artist

About Me

My paintings are about staging an alternate reality, the illusion of verisimilitude on the painted surface, filtered so that it expresses my unique vision. Though my paintings may appear photoreal, my goal is not to reproduce or document faithfully what I see, but to create the illusion of depth, a sense of presence, and a reality not found in photographs.

I try to use objects as a vehicle to express myself, arranged to convey a story. The objects I choose serve as vessels of memory and symbols for those who have made indelible marks upon my life. I select colours and composition intuitively to infuse my work with mood and mystery, remaining open to adaptation throughout the creative process.

I am the current President of the Royal Society of Miniature Painters, Sculptors and Gravers (RMS) and exhibit my miniature artwork annually in London. A British artist of Thai ancestry based in Essex, UK, I employ 16th-century Dutch painting methods with contemporary aesthetics.

Learn & Create

Learning Art

I offer adult workshops in realism and photorealism using a wide range of media, including graphite, charcoal, pastel, watercolours, gouache, acrylics, airbrushing, water-mixable oils, alkyd based oils and traditional oil paints.

Instruction covers multiple levels and subjects: learning to draw for beginners, drawing for intermediate and advanced levels, drawing portraits in charcoal, perspective, colour theory, portraits in pastels, portraits in acrylics, portraits in oils and many more. Available through workshops, demonstrations, YouTube and private tuition.

Drawing & Charcoal

From beginner fundamentals to advanced technique — learn to see and render with graphite, charcoal, and compressed charcoal.

Portraits in Pastels

Capture likeness and character using soft pastels, building layers of colour and tone to bring faces to life.

Oil Painting

Explore traditional oil techniques from alla prima to glazing, using methods drawn from 16th-century Dutch masters.

Watercolour

Work with the unpredictable beauty of watercolour and gouache — learning to control and embrace the medium.

Colour Theory

Understand the science and poetry of colour — mixing, temperature, and the relationships that make paintings sing.

Private Tuition

One-to-one sessions tailored entirely to your goals, skill level, and preferred medium. Learn at your own pace.
